What are the main predictive maintenance strategies for industrial IoT applications?
Second Mind
Based on your research documents, industrial IoT predictive maintenance strategies generally fall into four categories:

1. Condition-based monitoring - Using real-time sensor data to monitor equipment health indicators and detect anomalies before failures occur.

2. Time-series forecasting - Applying machine learning to historical data to predict when components will reach critical wear points or failure thresholds.

3. Digital twin simulation - Creating virtual replicas of physical assets to model performance under various conditions and predict maintenance needs.

4. Failure mode analysis - Using root cause analysis techniques and ML classification to identify specific failure patterns and their precursors.

Most modern implementations use a hybrid approach combining multiple strategies tailored to specific equipment and operational environments.
